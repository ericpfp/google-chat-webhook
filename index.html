A Google Chat simple webhook sender. Forked from Zua

HOW TO USE:
1. Go to the Google Chat/Hangout you would like to use. (Only Google Chat users can see your webhook messages)
2. Click on the name of the group on the top.
3. Click "Manage webhooks" and add a webhook.
4. Create a name and optionally, a Avatar URL.
5. Copy the Webhook URL to the webpage and put it in the "Webhook URL" spot.
6. Type your message and press enter.
7. If your webhook is incorrect it should give an error message.
